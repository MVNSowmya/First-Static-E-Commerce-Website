17 Years' Solved Papers Andhra Pradesh EAMCET - Engineering is designed to fulfill the students’ examination needs as a concise revision and a practice tool. The book effectively covers all the concepts of the syllabi for the Andhra Pradesh EAMCET Engineering Entrance test, which is conducted by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technical University.

The book contains the questions and answers of the last 17 years’ Solved Papers of EAMCET Engineering Test. The solutions given to the previous years’ exam questions are detailed and explained in a lucid manner so as to ensure maximum and easy comprehension. Thus, the answers include tables, diagrams, chemical equations, and figures.

Boasting of ample questions and concepts, the book is so designed that it provides the perfect tool for engineering aspirants to hone their skills and practice for the JNTU entrance exam. The previous years’ papers range from EAMCET, Engineering Solved Paper, 1997 till the latest EAMCET, Engineering Solved Paper of 2013

The third edition of 17 Years' Solved Papers Andhra Pradesh EAMCET - Engineering, was published by Arihant Publishers in 2013. The book is available in paperback.

Key Features:

Each previous year’s paper is divided into three sections, namely Chemistry, Mathematics, and Physics, with objective-type questions.

The more difficult concepts have been explained with the help of relevant support theories.

The book also contains an introduction to the EAMCET.
